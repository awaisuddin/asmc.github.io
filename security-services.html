Alarm Monitoring & Fire Prevention

Integration software from Advanced Security Company for Security Surveillance allows your intrusion detection systems to work together with other security systems. Cameras Security, IP Security Center, and AEOS are some of our integration and management solutions.

Depending on your risks and business operations, several aspects make up an intrusion detection system. The detectors and alarm monitoring to a central station are the system's most important components.

Detectors

Intruder detectors are made to detect intruders as soon as feasible. The sooner an intruder is discovered, the more likely he will be apprehended before achieving his aim. There are several types of detectors available. Each has its unique sensing/operational concept, application, and benefits and drawbacks.
