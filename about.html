ABOUT US

The Advanced Security Company for Security Surveillance is led by dedicated safety and security professionals. We have evolved into a serious and valued partner within the Dutch (and international) security business thanks to our 15 years of expertise and service-oriented, professional attitude. Advanced Security Company for Security Surveillance sets itself apart by creating security solutions that make a real impact, reduce risks, and are tailored to your individual needs. We employ carefully selected, innovative goods, which we supplement with custom-made components as appropriate. As a result, you're helping to keep your company running.

MISSION

WITH ADVANCED SECURITY COMPANY FOR SECURITY SURVEILLANCE'S HOLISTIC SECURITY SOLUTIONS, OUR CLIENTS HAVE DEMONSTRABLE CONTROL.

Advanced Security Company for Security Surveillance wishes to relieve its clients of their concerns about safety and security. To that purpose, we detect threats and transform them into real, effective solutions. Solutions that help you manage your physical hazards while also giving you measurable control. We accept responsibility for achieving your security objectives.

VISION


We are dedicated to always improving the services we deliver to our customers. We aspire to fulfill all of our objectives and surpass our clients' expectations by investing in and growing our most valuable asset, our people. Our goal is to win our clients' trust by providing the highest quality security services in Saudi Arabia, based on our dedication to high standards.
